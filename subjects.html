My Academic Interests

Mathematics

I love math because it's like a universal language that explains how the world works. Solving complex problems feels like unlocking secrets of the universe. My favorite areas are algebra and geometry because they help me understand patterns and shapes in everything.

Science

Science fascinates me because it answers "why" and "how" questions about everything around us. I especially enjoy physics and chemistry experiments that show scientific principles in action.

Engineering

Engineering combines math and science to solve real-world problems. I like how it uses creativity to design solutions that improve people's lives.
